Questions

Media Converter FAQ

Are my files safe?

PMW Media Converter is designed to process files in your browser when possible, so many basic conversions do not need to upload your files to our server.

Where are my files stored?

For browser-based conversion, your files stay on your device and are not stored by PMW Visuals. If any feature uses server processing in the future, the page should clearly explain it.

Is PMW Media Converter free?

Yes. You can convert files for free with daily limits. Premium users may get higher limits and extra features.

What file types are supported?

The converter supports common image, video, and audio formats such as JPG, PNG, WEBP, MP4, WEBM, MP3, and WAV, depending on browser support and the selected tab.

Why are some formats not available for my file?

Some conversions depend on browser support, file type, and available encoding features. Not every browser supports every format.

Will converting reduce quality?

It can. Some formats and lower quality settings may reduce file quality, while others may keep more detail but create larger files.

Is converting the same as compressing?

No. Converting changes the file type. Compression mainly reduces file size. Use Image Compressor when your main goal is smaller images.

Is converting the same as resizing?

No. Converting changes the format. Resizing changes image width and height. Use Image Resizer to change dimensions.

Can I convert images for websites?

Yes. Formats like WEBP, JPG, and PNG are commonly used for websites, previews, thumbnails, and wallpapers.

Can I convert audio files?

Yes, if the audio tab and selected output format are supported by your browser.

Can I convert video files?

Yes, if the video tab and selected output format are supported. Large videos may take longer or may require premium limits in the future.

Will PMW Visuals see my uploaded file?

For browser-based conversion, the file is processed on your device and is not manually reviewed or accessed by PMW Visuals.
