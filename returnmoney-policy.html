Return Money Policy

This policy explains how PMW Visuals handles cancellation and return money requests for premium access.

Cancel Anytime

You may cancel your PMW Visuals premium access at any time. Cancellation stops future access or future renewal where applicable, but it does not automatically guarantee a money return.

Manual Review Required

All return money requests are reviewed manually by PMW Visuals. We may check account activity, payment details, premium access history, and download records before making a decision.

When Money May Be Returned

A money return may be approved only if the user has not downloaded, accessed, saved, copied, or used any premium wallpaper or premium content after purchase.

When Money Will Not Be Returned

Money will not normally be returned if premium content has already been downloaded, opened, used, copied, shared, or otherwise accessed through the premium account.

Processing Time

If a return money request is approved, processing time may depend on the payment provider, bank, or payment method used for the purchase.

Contact

To request a manual review, contact PMW Visuals through the contact options provided on the website and include your account email and payment reference if available.
